Dub resolves a short-link click through three runtime phases, all anchored in the edge middleware:

**1. Request handling** — A request hits `middleware.ts:35` (`middleware()`), which dispatches to `LinkMiddleware()` in `lib/middleware/link.ts:40`. This resolves the incoming short link, and `cacheDeepLinkClickData()` (`lib/middleware/utils/cache-deeplink-click-data.ts:10`) caches the click data for the matched link.

**2. Analytics tracking** — The click is recorded to Tinybird via `recordClick()` in `lib/tinybird/record-click.ts:28`, which logs the click event for analytics.

**3. Destination redirect** — The middleware issues the redirect to the link's destination with `NextResponse.redirect` (`lib/middleware/link.ts:77`, also surfaced at `middleware.ts:74`).

**Key files**
- `middleware.ts` — entrypoint dispatch + redirect
- `lib/middleware/link.ts` — link resolution and redirect (`LinkMiddleware`)
- `lib/middleware/utils/cache-deeplink-click-data.ts` — click-data caching
- `lib/tinybird/record-click.ts` — analytics tracking

Flow: `middleware()` → `LinkMiddleware()` → `cacheDeepLinkClickData()` → `recordClick()` (Tinybird) → `NextResponse.redirect` to the destination.
